Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ClassSnap (the "App") collects the following information:

  • Photo Data: Photos taken by users with the camera are stored locally on the device.
  • Timetable Data: Subject names, class times, and other timetable information entered by users are stored locally on the device.
  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA): With user consent, we collect advertising identifiers to deliver personalized ads.

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing core app functionality (photo organization, timetable management)
  • Delivering advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Improving the app and enhancing user experience

3. Data Storage and Sharing

  • Local Storage: All photos and timetable data are stored locally on the user's device and are not transmitted to external servers.
  • Third-Party Services: This app uses Google AdMob to deliver advertisements. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (IDFA) for ad delivery. For more details, please see the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Deletion

Users can delete their data by:

  • Deleting individual photos or subjects within the app
  • Uninstalling the app, which will remove all local data

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data. However, please understand that no method of internet transmission or device storage is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. When changes are made, we will update this page and note the last update date.
